About

I've had ideas for writing for quite a while now its finally time for me to act on them.

I made this website to keep myself accountable to continueing to grow as a writer. My goal is a collection of one hundred short stories, not all of them will be published but hopefully all will be written. I enjoy writing science fiction, topics about future advancements, and mental health related episodes. I also write poems and lyrics because they interest me.

My influences are Fyodor Dostoyevsky, Isaac Asimov, and James Dashner to name a few. I recently discovered the public library's catalogue on the libby app!

I will try to keep this updated as often as I can.
